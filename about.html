About Us

Plumbing is our focus

We won’t sell you expensive gas or cooking solutions, we will just provide you the best plumbing solutions. Our team of experts are available for all your plumbing and drainage issues, Auckland wide.

We can help you change your taps, unblock your toilet or install your new bathroom, the choice is yours. Customer satisfaction is number 1 for us and we want to be your first choice, every time. One of our qualified plumbers can help you with all your plumbing:

  • Blocked toilets
  • Commercial work
  • Residential work
  • Kitchen and bathroom renovations
  • Installation of sinks and vanities
  • Leaks in laundries
  • Installation of baths
  • Leaking water mains
  • Leaking roofs and gutters
  • Installation or repair of spouting and downpipes
  • Leaking pipes
  • Improving hot water pressure
  • Leaking taps
  • Overflowing hot water cylinders
  • Blocked drains
  • Dripping or Leaky taps
  • Plumbing in dishwashers
  • Installing water filtration system
  • Replacing water filters
