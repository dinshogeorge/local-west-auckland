Benefits of having water filter for your family

Water Filter Repair & Replacement

When you have a water pump malfunction, West Auckland Plumbers is never far away.
We have the people, the knowledge and the spare parts required to fix - repair or replace- the broken down or malfunctioning water pump at your place.
Any time of the day or night, don’t let the water pump trouble wait and become a bigger problem to solve. Call us right away and we’ll be there with our van, well equipped with all tools and pumps to resolve, all within the budget, of course.
All water filters come with a life span and it varies according to the type & brand. Commonly, the water filters installed in a home can filter up to 60 gallons of water. It is the responsibility of the homeowners to keep a track of the time when the filter was installed or replaced. If you forget to replace the filter at regular intervals the water can get dirtier. It is best to replace the water filter every 4 months to have clean & pure water. Our replacement cost starts from $400 depending on the type of filter you have.

Check out the signs for replacing the water filter

  • 1. Bad smell or odors
  • 2. Floating bits
  • 3. Slippery water
  • 4. Metallic Taste
  • 5. Color Change
  • 6. Low water pressure

If you are experiencing any of the above systems with your water filter, call us right away. One of our plumbers will reach you at the earliest to replace your filter.
